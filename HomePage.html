Take Part in Canopy Walks

Enjoy the adventure of Canopy walking and meet new people, although some people enjoy the peace of walking alone around their homes. Rambling Club brings people together for a healthy and an adventurous walk, whiles spending time with friends and family.


Join Power Walking

This is a quicker brisk walk, you can actually burn a lot of calories when you take part in this walk. If you do not want to jog on a weekend you can consider power walking with us. Also, if you want to lose weight this is the right walk for you. Enjoy walking in groups and losing weight as well.

Marathon Walking

Currently the biggest Marathon in Ghana is the Accra Marathon. Walk and enjoy the beautiful view of the entire city of Accra. Famous ones around the world include Tokyo and London Marathons.

What to Bring Along

Bring strong boots for walks, gloves and a hat. Stuff your backpack with bottles of water for the journey and other relevant things you will need for the walk. First aid and meals will be provided for you. Click Join!
