Walking Grade Kilometres Brief Details Suitable For
"A" 4 Grade A is an easy 4km walk beginning from the starting point of the Kakum canopy to the end of the canopy. It is on woodland trail and has grits paths, it also involves a short steep climb up the canopy. The canopy swings as you walk on it.  Its an adventurous walk with great views. The path taken to the canopy is a water logged area so it is likely to be muddy. It is a walk suitable for regular walkers. Everybody
"B" 22 This is a 20km walk from Adenta to Aburi Gardens on Aburi Mountain. The walk starts from the Adenta Pharmacy and head towards Timora Gardens then following a straight path, we walk across to Zanzibar. The walk continues towards Lodge Tavern and accros to Aburi Girl's SHS and then finally to Aburi Garden. It includes a long steep climb to Aburi. The roads might be very slippery due to the recent rains. It is not an easy walk, it is a long walk on the mountain. Fit and no experience
"C" 41 It is a very long hilly walk on tarmac trail, the walk starts from Tema Beach road and head towards the Black Star Square, the walk continues towards Accra Academy all the way to MCCarthy Hill. More tarmac trail takes us along the Atta Mills road to Bortianor then finally to Krokrobite. Approximately 41kilometers, the roads might be slippery and this walk is not suitable for everyone. Very fit and experienced
